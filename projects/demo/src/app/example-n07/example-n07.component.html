Carousel

You can also use the redZoom directive, for example with a DIV tag. This ability is useful when your images are in a carousel, and they can change their position when swiping.

<div [redZoom]="currentImage.full" [redZoomThumb]="currentImage.thumbnail" > <owl-carousel-o> <ng-container *ngFor="let image of images"> <ng-template carouselSlide> <img [src]="image.thumbnail"> </ng-template> </ng-container> </owl-carousel-o> </div>
